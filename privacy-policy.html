Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 2025

Introduction

Thank you for using the Scholarships, Internships & Opportunities Chrome extension. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

Our extension does not collect or transmit any personal or sensitive information. The only data it stores is the information you choose to save, such as:

  • The title, URL, and deadline of opportunities like scholarships, internships, and competitions.

This data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage.local API and is not shared with any third parties.

Permissions Used

The extension requests the following permissions to function:

  • Storage: To locally save opportunities you add.
  • Tabs & activeTab: To open links in new tabs and interact with content on the active tab when saving opportunities.
  • Host permissions for givemechallenge.com: To access opportunity pages where the save functionality is provided.

These permissions are strictly necessary to provide the core functionality and are not used for tracking or advertising.

Data Usage

All data remains on your device and is used only to help you manage saved opportunities and deadlines. We do not transmit, share, or store your data on external servers.

Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure that your data remains secure by keeping all saved information within your browser's local storage. We do not use or integrate with third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.
